INTRODUCTION

Cornell Appropriate Use Criteria

The Weill-Cornell head imaging guideline covers a number of common head conditions including headache, minor head trauma and syncope. The guidelines are based upon the American College of Emergency Physician recommendations for imaging as well as their own expert opinion.
