VERSION NOTES

Changes made to this version:

  • Data enabled: Decision Support Server (DSS) uses data from the EHR to determine which clinical condition applies. DSS asks questions only if data is not already provided.
  • Contraindications: contraindication information can be passed in from the EHR, and DSS responds accordingly. If any specific contraindication is not mentioned in the incoming data, DSS will ask about the contraindications if it is needed.
  • DSS version 5.1.3, or later required.
  • Configuration of advice text: set {advice.text.addendum} to a string of text to be appended to the advice text. For example, "If there are any questions on the most appropriate examination for your patient, please contact radiology consultation at ###-###-#### or abc@med.hospital.edu." This is an optional "config item" in the domain deployment rule.
  • This portal variation is a translation of RS001WCI v5, such that there are no BPA questions, and all questions are mandatory.

INTRODUCTION

Pulmonary embolism (PE) is a common differential diagnosis for patients who present with shortness of breath and chest pain.

Risk models such as the modified Wells criteria in combination with D-dimer results can assist in the identification patients who will likely benefit from a CT scan and avoid imaging for those who will not.
